HMB

HMB is an active metabolite of Leucine (a branched-chain amino acid) that helps prevent muscle protein breakdown. HMB signals muscle cells to preserve the existing muscle.

  • Prevents muscle protein breakdown
  • Increases strength gains
  • Improves training endurance
  • Aids in muscle repair and recovery reducing recovery time needed between training sessions
  • Increases lean muscle mass
  • Works well with creatine to provide performance boost during training
