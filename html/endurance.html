Beta Alanine

Beta-alanine is the building block of carnosine, a molecule that helps buffer acid in muscles, increasing physical performance.

  • Enhance muscluar endurance
  • Improve moderate to high intensity cardivascular exercise
  • Offers protection from lactic acid produced from exercise
  • reduces fatigue and increases time to exhaustion
  • Promotes lean mass
  • Aids in fat loss

Supplementation of Beta Alanine is not timing dependent. To avoid paresthesia (tinging feeling) we recommend splitting the daily 5 gram dose into two 2.5 gram servings pre and post workout.

L-Citrulline DL-Malate 2:1

L-Citrulline is an amino acid that is turned into L-arginine which then produces Nitric Oxide. Malic acid is combined with L-Citrulline to increase its absorption rate. Used to reduce fatigue and improve endurance for both aerobic and anaerobic prolonged exercise.

  • Increase in muscle ATP production
  • absorbed better than L-arginine
  • Decreases fatigue during exercise
  • Reduces post workout muscle soreness
  • Increases nitic oxide
  • Increases training volume
  • Increaes blood flow
  • Increases Growth Hormone secreted from exercise

Branch Chain Amino Acids 2:1:1

Branch Chain Amino Acids are the three essential amino acids Leucine, Isoleucine, and Valine. They will help in increasing muscle protein syntehsis and prevent fatigue.

  • Increased time to exhaustion
  • Promote muscle protein synthesis and increase muscle growth over time
  • Increase in fat oxidation
  • Decrease fatigue
  • Prevents muscle breakdown sustaining hard earned muscle.

Beet Root extract

Beet root extract is a supplement high in Nitrates which will be converted into Nitric Oxide once consumed to aid with exercise performance.

  • Reduces the oxygen required for exercise leading to greater endurance and improving time to exhaustion.
  • Increases Nitric Oxide levels
  • Lowers blood pressure
  • preserves exercise performance during hypoxia (deficiency in amount of oxygen in muscle tissue)
  • Increases performance on short term high intensity exercises and prolonged cardiovascular exercises
