Energy and Focus

These are the supplements that will give you the energy you need to power through your workouts after a long day

Caffeine

Caffeine is a popular performance and stimulatory anti-sleep compound extracted from coffee beans. Caffeine Anhydrous is simply powdered caffeine. Users

  • Fat burner.
  • Increase power output.
  • Fatigue fighter
  • Increase in aerobic performance.
  • Boost metabolism
  • Increase endurance performance
  • appetite suppressant
  • Increase in training volume
  • Reduces the users perception of pain allowing to push past pain barriers and train harder

Select caffeine levels based on your tolerence to caffeine. 100 milligrams of caffeine is equivalent to one cup of coffee

L-Theanine

L-Theanine is a non-dietary amino acid found in green tea (Camellia sinensis), occuring naturally alongside caffeine. It is synergistic with caffeine for promoting a state of increased attention and focus while reducing stimulant side effects.

  • Heightened sense of focus and concentration when paired with caffeine
  • Increases energy levels
  • Enhances caffeines benefits
  • Eliminates anxiety, jitters, and the crash experienced with caffeine consumption
  • Elevates mood

Custom Pre-Workout

The typical Caffeine to Theanine stack is a 1:2 ratio. For every 100 mg of caffeine look to pair it with 200 milligrams of Theanine.

Acetyl-L-Carnitine

Acetyl-L-Carnitine is an amino acid that transports fat into the mitochondria of cells where they can be oxidized and used as fuel to provide energy (ATP).

  • Limits fat gains and promotes lean muscle mass
  • Uses fat as energy
  • Reduces physical and mental fatigue
  • Minimizes lactate production created during training
  • Decreaces post workout muscle soreness
  • Increase in anaerobic cardivascular exercise
  • Boosts blood flow to muscles
  • Provides intense focus
  • Aids in recovery from exercise

Daily dose can range from 500 milligrams to 2.5 grams. We suggest splitting into two doses pre and post workout.

L-Tyrosine

Tyrosine is a non-essential amino acid that is a building block of protein found in food sources such as spinach, avocado, turkey and chicken . Tyrosine is used to produce noradrenaline and dopamine which can aid exercise performance through energy and focus.

  • Increases alertness
  • Enhances mental focus and mood
  • provides energy for intense exercise
  • Enhances the benefits of caffeine

Synephrine-HCL

Synephrine is an active ingredient in bitter orange that can increase metabolic rates, increase lipolysis (the deconstruction of fats for energy), and provide energy without increasing blood pressure or heart rate.

  • Increases metaboilic rate and provides energy without raising blood pressure or heart rate
  • Increases lipolysis(the deconstruction of fats for energy)
  • Works synergistically with caffeine to increase their fat loss properties
  • Appetite suppressant
  • provides energy without feeling jittery or wired.
